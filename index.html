Our Services

Repairs

We can service all types of computers such as Acer, Dell, HP, Compaq, Toshiba, NEC, ASUS, Custom Systems etc.

Data

Our experienced staff members have tools that enable us to retrieve data from damaged storage devices (internal and external) and can replace the old one with a brand new hard drive while we're at it.

Virus Removal

If you notice that your internet experience is slow, with the occasional unprovoked pop-up window appearing - you could have a virus that is leaching your private information to the world!
